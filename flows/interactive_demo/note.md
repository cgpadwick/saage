Hi Chis! Thanks for chatting with me today. You asked for a fun fact about space animals — did you know that a pair of tortoises were the first living creatures to travel around the Moon? In September 1968, the Soviet Union launched Zond 5 carrying two steppe tortoises, along with some worms and flies, and they safely returned to Earth after looping around the Moon. That means tortoises beat the Apollo 8 astronauts (who orbited the Moon in December 1968) by a few months! It's a delightfully slow-and-steady-wins-the-race kind of story.
